Dress Alterations

Custom alterations involve tailoring services that adjust and modify clothing to meet an individual’s specific measurements and style preferences. Tailors and seamstresses offer these personalized services, focusing on garment modifications such as fabric tailoring, dress adjustments, suit tailoring, and more complex clothing repairs.

Leather Repair

A leather repair patch is simply an adhesive-backed piece of leather that can be used to repair cracks, scratches, and tears on sofas, chairs, and car seats. To use it, measure and cut the leather patch into the size and shape you want according to the defect on your leather item.

Wedding Gown Alterations

Wedding dress alterations are the changes your seamstress or dress stylist will make to ensure your gown fits like a glove. To fit you well, your wedding dress should support and accentuate your bust and complement the shape of your figure. The skirt should be long enough to cover your shoes, but not so long that you’re tripping over it.
